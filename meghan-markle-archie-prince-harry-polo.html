Meghan Markle, Archie, and Prince Harry Have a Moment at Polo Match

July 26, 2024
blog
  • Meghan Markle brought her son Archie to his first public appearance at a charity polo match today.
  • Husband Prince Harry was playing against Prince William in the game.
  • The family of three shared a sweet moment after the competition.

Archie Mountbatten-Windsor, the two-month-old son of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle, made his first public appearance today as he attended a charity polo match with family. Just days after his private christening, the little royal was seen swaddled in his mother's arms as his dad played against uncle Prince William on the field. Although the infant spent the game on the sidelines, he and Meghan reunited with Harry at the end of the game for a sweet family moment.

Harry, still in his uniform and looking winded from the game, caught up with his wife and son off the field. He was seen putting his hand on Meghan's back as she held their firstborn in a yellow-trimmed printed blanket.

The Duchess of Sussex wore a loose-fitting khaki green midi dress by Lisa Marie Fernandez and a pair of shades for a laid-back summer look. Her baby boy wore a white onesie when he wasn't all wrapped up.

The Sussexes recently made a family appearance for Archie's christening at Windsor Castle over the weekend. A stunning portrait from the event shows the proud new parents beaming at their son, who was born on May 6.

CHRIS ALLERTON//Getty ImagesMeghan, wearing Dior, Archie, and Harry pose together after the little one’s christening.

Although the duke and duchess have been keeping their newborn out of the public eye, that hasn't stopped them from gushing over how he's changed their lives. During Archie's public debut days after his birth, Harry told the press, "We are just so thrilled to have our own bundle of joy and to be able to spend some precious time as he slowly starts to grow up."
