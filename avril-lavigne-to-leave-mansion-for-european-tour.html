Avril Lavigne is poised to leave $7m mansion after split from Mod Son

July 04, 2024
blog

Avril Lavigne has had a turbulent time lately, with her recent break-up with ex-fiancé Mod Sun and sightings soon after with Tyga - and now the singer is set to leave her waterside $7.8million Malibu home. However, it's all for good reason!

The singer is about to embark on a European wide tour, and she shared all of her dates on Instagram Stories for her fans to see. On April 12 Avril will be lighting up the stage in Paris and after travelling to Milan, Prague and Vienna among others, she'll finish up with multiple nights in London.

After her last gig on 10 May, it will likely be home time for Avril who will be able to retreat back to her gorgeous residence. 

While the star likes to keep her personal life under wraps, we do know a little about the amazing property that the Canadian singer purchased back in 2020. 

The single-story home has breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean – and it's just steps to the beach – how dreamy! Avril's home features an open-plan design with hardwood floors, custom woodwork, and exposed beam ceilings.

The master bedroom has its own wing of the house, complete with spa-style bathroom. The other three bedrooms are just as beautifully appointed with modern décor throughout. 

While dating rumours have been swirling around Avril, Mod Son has thrown himself into his music.
