The inspiration behind Tendencies stems from the band members’ personal experiences with addiction and mental health. Through the song, Hollywood Undead seeks to share their own stories of struggle and encourage others to face their inner demons head-on.

The overall message portrayed in Tendencies is one of resilience and the drive for self-improvement. It encourages listeners to face their insecurities, seek help when needed, and ultimately find the strength to overcome their destructive tendencies.

Tendencies resonates with fans due to its relatable and honest portrayal of the human condition. The vulnerable lyrics and powerful melodies create a deep emotional connection, allowing listeners to feel understood and supported in their own struggles.

The title “Tendencies” refers to the inclination or predisposition towards certain behaviors. It highlights the recurring patterns of self-destructive behavior that can arise in individuals dealing with addiction or mental health issues.
