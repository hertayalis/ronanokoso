The Season 8 cast of Bachelor in Paradise will include Lace Morris

August 01, 2024
blog

In season eight of Bachelor in Paradise, Lace Morris makes her third appearance on Bachelor Nation. She debuted in The Bachelor Season 20 with Ben Higgins in 2016.

She left the house after only three weeks, however, she returned as part of BiP Season 3 and left there engaged to Grant Kemp. To locate The One for herself, she has returned to the same program. Mexico-based Bachelor in Paradise stars well-known franchise alums who have previously competed in a Bachelor Nation series.

On Bachelor in Paradise, is Lace Morris’ third attempt fortunate?

Selling a dream is more significant to Lace than selling a property because she believes that home is where the heart is. The passionate and knowledgeable realtor believes that her client’s needs should come first.

The stylish fitness fanatic and outdoor enthusiast aggressively practice honesty and integrity in her daily interactions and interpersonal relationships. Lace previously went on Bachelor in Paradise, where she fell in love with Grant Kemp, a former contestant on The Bachelorette, right away and got engaged. Viewers were left wondering whether the two were prepared to be engaged because their relationship was portrayed to be rocky during the entire show.

The pair split up less than a year into their relationship. When asked about her relationship, Lace told Entertainment Tonight that it was mutually decided that the moment was not appropriate to end things.

She remarked:

We both have challenges to overcome, therefore we are unable to genuinely give each other what we need at this time.

Lace continued:

We have such a strong bond and it’s strange that we can’t make this work because the chemistry is there. But that is simply impossible.

She claimed that her relationship was “passion, not love” on the Ben and Ashley I Almost Famous podcast. To take part in Bachelor in Paradise Season 8 and give love a third shot, the 32-year-old realtor will now travel to Mexico.

Additional details regarding the program

To give viewers a second opportunity at finding love in a situation that is different from what they are used to, Bachelor in Paradise is ready to bring back some of their greatest fan-favorite rejections. Wells Adams, who recently wed Sarah Hyland of Modern Family, is making a beach comeback with his renowned beverages.

In contrast to the last season of the show, which included a rotating panel of hosts, Jesse Palmer will assume sole responsibility for hosting BiP Season 8. Watch what transpires when adversaries from the same season interact with strangers from other seasons. Will there be sparks, or will the lovely beach burn?
