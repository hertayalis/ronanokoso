Kylie Jenner Attends Vanity Fair's Oscars After-Party In Maison Margiela With Kendall Jenner &amp; Gigi

May 26, 2024
blog

Awards season has been unfolding over the past few weeks, and on Sunday (March 12) evening, one of the biggest ceremonies of all finally took place – the 2023 Academy Awards. Though the show itself is mostly for those who work in the film industry, a few musical talents were on the red carpet as well. Among them was Rihanna, whose baby bump was proudly on display. Of course, the subsequent after-parties are where plenty of models and socialites flocked in the hours following the Oscars, including Kylie Jenner.

The mother of two was one of the many famous beauties at Vanity Fair's annual soirée. Her on-again-off-again baby daddy, Travis Scott, was nowhere to be seen, though Jenner did have a few of her closest friends to dance the night away with in her custom Maison Margiela gown. Earlier this year, she wore another stunning look from the fashion house while attending their Paris Fashion Week runway.

Kylie Jenner Stuns in Maison Margiela

Kylie Jenner attends the 2023 Vanity Fair Oscar Party Hosted By Radhika Jones at Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts on March 12, 2023, in Beverly Hills, California. (Photo by Leon Bennett/FilmMagic)

Kendall Jenner joined her little sister last night, looking as stunning as ever in a Jean Paul Gaultier gown which perfectly hugged her hips. After the party, the 818 Tequila founder continued to fuel romance rumours with Bad Bunny by leaving in the same town car as him. Nothing has been confirmed between the two just yet, though the increasing frequency of their time together is very telling.

Others hanging out with the makeup mogul included Gigi Hadid, Emily Ratajkowski, and Stassie Karanikolaou. Throughout their evening, the group posed for several sweet pictures together.
