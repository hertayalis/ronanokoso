Who is Sky Brown?

Japanese-British Sky Brown is the world’s youngest professional skateboarder. Brown is renowned for her skateboard competitions.

She is also the winner of Dancing With the Stars.

Sky Brown- Birth Age, Family

Sky Brown was born on the 12th of July 2008 in Miyazaki, Japan. Her father Stu Brown is of British ancestry and her mother Mieko is of Japanese heritage.

Sky Brown has a younger brother, Ocean.

Sky Brown- Career

– As a child, she was inspired by her father to skate. Besides his father, she also followed the skateboarders Leticia Bufoni and Alana Smith. At the age of four, she started riding the skateboard following her father.

– First, Sky Brown borrowed her father’s skateboard without permission and started riding around her home space. In a very small time interval, she became a pro in skateboarding.

– In 2016, she became the youngest contender for the Vans US Open Pro Series 2016. She started traveling the world in order to compete in the skateboard competition. In February 2016, she earned Gold at the Simple session in Tallin, Estonia.

– After two years, Sky Brown won the reality dancing show, Dancing With the Stars. In the show, she was partnered with the dancer, JT Church. Alan Bersten was their mentor for them at the show.

– Subsequently, she started endorsing brands like Nike, Polly Pocket, and Tech Deck.

Olympics

In 2019, Sky Brown was named to be in the British skateboard team along with the team members like Alex Decunha, Sam Beckett, Alex Hallford, and Jordan Thackeray.

If the team is qualified, she will be the youngest Olympics member for Great Britain. Apart from that, she will be the youngest female in Olympics history to compete at the Summer Olympics. She will be 12 years old when the game will begin in Tokyo, Japan.

Sky Brown’s Net Worth, Salary

Sky Brown has an estimated net worth of $5 million US. As a professional Skateboarder, her earnings are in the range of $12k to $120k US.

As a winner amount of Dancing With the Stars is in the range of $345k to $360k US. From her YouTube channel, her estimated yearly earnings are $1.6K – $25.8K US.

Apart from that, Sky Brown also earns through a brand promotion like Nike. As the global ambassador of Polly Pocket, her average salary is $40k-$50k US.

Sky’s Height and Weight

Sky Brown is a brown-eyed brunette. Her height is 4 feet and 5 inches and weighs around 47kg.

Sky Brown- Social Media, Philanthropist

Sky Brown has 447k followers on Instagram and 202k on Facebook. Her mother manages her Instagram account. She also has a YouTube channel along with her brother. The channel has 79.1f subscribers with 11 million views.

Through her Social media account, she is selling her limited edition poster for $3 dollars in order to raise funds for the non-profit organization, Skateistan. 

The organization uses the fund to empower the children of Afghanistan, Cambodia, and South Africa through skateboarding and education.
